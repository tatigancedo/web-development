About

Boards of Canada is an ambient and electronic band made up of two Scottish brothers. Their music has been a major inspiration for many electronic musicians today. They've also been an inspiration for me since 2005. No other band can take me to either my childhood or a place I've never been before. Perception of time is lost when hearing their songs.
